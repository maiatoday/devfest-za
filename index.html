About

GDG DevFest 2021 season is here! This is a series of community-led events, where you can learn the essentials and best practices of various Google technologies and products: Android, Web, Google Cloud Platform and more. This is the fourth edition of our successful conference, previously held in Johannesburg, Cape Town, and virtually. This year we are aiming to replicate its success online! And best of all - attendance is free! NOTE: DevFest is jointly organized by several South African GDG chapters. In addition to signing up for DevFest, please remember to join the chapter closest to you to receive future invites.
